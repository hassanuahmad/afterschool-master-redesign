FAQ

I understand that non-English speakers are required to submit an English proficiency test when they apply for university in Canada. Does your school offer an IELTS or TOEFL test preparation program for international students?

That is correct. Any international students from non-English speaking countries, who have been in Canada less than 4 or 5 years, should prove his or her ability with an English proficiency test either with IELTS or TOEFL. The overall IELTS on the Academic Module should be in the range of 6.5 to 7.5 points, depending on the requirements of each university or college (with a minimum of 6.0 in each section of Speaking, Listening, Reading and Writing), while the IBT-based TOEFL should be 6.5 or more as a minimum (Normally, universities and colleges in Canada require paper-based TOEFL scores be 550 to 600 points). It is not surprising that Canada’s top 4 universities require higher scores of an English proficiency test from non-English speaking students. The top 4 schools include the University of Toronto, the University of British Columbia, McGill University and the University of Montreal.

We provide an English proficiency test preparation course including IELTS and TOEFL for the Academic Module to international students who are entering into university or college in Canada. We use the Barrons or the Cambridge Program for the IELTS preparation course. As our teachers’ profiles explain, we have a number of highly qualified and substantially experienced English instructors experienced in teaching ESL and IELTS at St. Clair College and overseas for many years with the Ontario Teacher Certification.

I am a newcomer from India and a 31-year old man who has an interest in applying for permanent residence in Canada. Do you offer any IELTS preparation course for an immigration purpose?

Yes, we offer an IELTS preparation course for the General Training Module as well to those who are preparing for permanent residence or citizenship in Canada. You are required to prove that you have your IELTS Test score on average 6.0 points or more (equivalent to Canada Language Benchmarks 7.0 points) in each section according to the Canada PR Express Entry Program.

I wonder if your school will provide any assistance for my son such as supplementary Math lessons. As I am a full-time worker, I would like to ask if your school can provide pickup service for my son from his school to your afterschool program

Thanks for your interest in our afterschool programs. We are happy to help your son in particular with the subject of Math. We have a highly qualified teacher who has been experienced in teaching at public school for 10 years as an Ontario Certified Teacher. We will have your child take an assessment first and offer him a tailored program meeting the needs of your son. We have lessons for other subjects, such as Science, available for elementary and high school students. When it comes to your concerns on transportation, we are delighted to provide pickup service to your child from his school to our place for a nominal rate.

English is a great challenge to my children who are experiencing difficulty in terms of reading and writing. I heard that you are offering supplementary afterschool lessons to low-grade students as well. I also heard that you provide us with a free assessment and a free consultation. More importantly, I am curious to know how much you will charge for my children’s tuition. Yes, we offer supplementary lessons for all subjects including English, Math and Science for elementary and high school students, not to mention our tailored assistance for Reading and Writing. We offer a very competitive rate to our students. The rate for high school students is slightly different from the one for elementary students. Our tuition rate ranges from $22 to $33 per session based on a typical 45-minute lecture. A special class is sometimes available upon request by clients. The lecture length for a special class varies. We also offer you an assessment and a consultation free of charge. Please do not hesitate to call us to slot your reservation for your child.
