Free Consultation


We provide a consultation service for free of charge to any parents and students. We meet with your child to find out what weaknesses or strengthsthe student has, if the student has any fears or barriers or not and how the student is inspired or encouraged. The consultation follows an assessment to diagnose his or her academic abilities

Free Assessment


Finishing the consultation process, we conduct a free assessment for the student to determine the level of his or her academic skills. The assessment covers all key components of English, including reading, speaking, listening, writing and vocabulary if he or she is taking English, for example. We issue an assessment statement, which will be a good resource to compare with what he or she will achieve later

Report Cards


Our instructors assign some homework for your child on a daily basis. With this approach, the student will get used to studying independently and regularly. We will conduct a test to check with how he or she is performing at the end of each month and prepare a report card and hand it out to the student together with our monthly newsletters

Newsletters


We prepare our newsletters monthly to provide parents with school news including news of events and cultural activities. We will upload the same news to our homepage as well. We welcome any input or contributions from you if you would like to share any news with other parents and our community

Pickup Services


We provide pickup services if any of you do not have transportation available for your child, in particular from school to our afterschool program. We are happy to provide you with this service for a nominal charge. Please discuss your request with our Business Manager to slot yours into our main schedule
