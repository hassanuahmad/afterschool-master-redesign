Homestay Programs


We invite students from overseas to have a language and cultural experience in Windsor periodically.They prefer to have the option of homestay accommodations while they stay in Canada as it presents the perfect opportunity for them to access true Canadian culture. They go to public schools or private or Christian schools in Windsor. For this purpose it is important for us to find and maintain a number of decent host families. If you are interested in this amazing opportunity, please call us as the first step. We will meet you to conduct an interview and complete a police check in the recruitment process. You will be compensated for your services with an appropriate amount

Process to apply to be a host family:

  • Please apply to be a host family by filling out the application form
  • We meet the host family and conduct an interview
  • Please secure a criminal record check or a Vulnerable Sector Search (VSS) from the police station
  • We match a student with a host family and place the student in your home
  • You will be compensated for your service by the time when the student arrives

We Expect from You:

  • A friendly and comfortable environment
  • We meet the host family and conduct an interview
  • An English-speaking environment
  • A clean room, 3 nutritious meals including typical sandwich for lunch and snacks
  • Support and guidance whenever the student needs regarding academic or culture
